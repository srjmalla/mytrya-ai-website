Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our AI automation process and services.

What kinds of businesses do you work with?

We work with service-based businesses, professional services, healthcare providers, financial institutions, e-commerce companies, and manufacturing firms. From small businesses to enterprise-level organizations, we help any company looking to streamline operations and boost efficiency through intelligent automation.

How long does implementation usually take?

Simple automation projects typically take 2-4 weeks, while comprehensive AI transformations range from 6-12 weeks. We work in phases to deliver quick wins early, so you'll start seeing benefits within the first 30 days while we complete the full implementation.

Who owns the AI that you build for customers?

You own 100% of the AI solutions we build for your business. All custom code, configurations, and data models become your intellectual property. We provide complete documentation and can train your team to manage the systems independently if desired.

How does your pricing work?

We offer flexible pricing models: project-based fees for specific automations, monthly retainers for ongoing optimization, and success-based pricing tied to measurable results. Every engagement starts with a free assessment to provide transparent cost estimates based on your specific needs and expected ROI.

Do you offer courses or free training?

Yes! We provide comprehensive training for your team as part of every implementation. We also offer workshops and educational content to help businesses understand AI opportunities. Follow our YouTube channel and LinkedIn for free resources, tips, and industry insights.

What industries do you specialize in?

We have deep expertise in healthcare, professional services, finance, e-commerce, manufacturing, and SaaS companies. However, our automation frameworks are adaptable across industries. We focus on common business processes like data entry, customer service, reporting, and workflow optimization that exist in most sectors.

Do you build solutions from scratch or leverage tools?

We use a hybrid approach - combining proven tools and platforms with custom development as needed. This gives you faster implementation, lower costs, and enterprise-grade reliability while ensuring solutions are perfectly tailored to your unique processes and requirements.

How do I know if AI is a good fit for my business?

If your business handles repetitive tasks, processes large amounts of data, or struggles with manual workflows, AI automation can help. The best candidates have clear processes that consume significant time and resources. Our free assessment will quickly identify your biggest automation opportunities and potential ROI.
